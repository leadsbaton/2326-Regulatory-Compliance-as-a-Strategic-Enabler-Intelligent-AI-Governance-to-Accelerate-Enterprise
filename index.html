Building Better Data Products with AI, for AI

AI is only as good as the data it's built upon, and as the AI era continues to evolve, the organizations that succeed won't just be the ones that have more data – they'll have better data products. High-quality data products ensure that models are trained on reliable data and business decisions are grounded in trust and accuracy. Building better data products requires well-structured and governed data pipelines. It also requires systems that can leverage AI to learn, adapt and optimize – often in real-time – to continuously deliver fresh, trusted data.

To dive into the essentials for building better data products with AI – for AI, DBTA is hosting a special roundtable webinar on November 20th that will cover:

  • Establishing a data foundation of trust, reliability and governance
  • Streamlining data product development and scalability with AI
  • Eliminating bottlenecks through automation and active metadata
  • Moving from static data assets to dynamic engines of intelligence
  • Emerging trends in AI and data product innovation
